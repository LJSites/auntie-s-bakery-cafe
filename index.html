~ a darling little place ~

The kind of spot you tell people about.

Auntie's sits on Greenleaf Avenue in uptown Whittier — small, clean, sun-bright, with a quaint porch out front where two dogs are usually napping by 9 AM. The owner remembers your name. The kitchen sends out plates worth driving for.

Try the chicken & waffles. Try the salmon toast. Try the breakfast burrito. Wash it down with a vanilla iced coffee. You'll come back. People do.
